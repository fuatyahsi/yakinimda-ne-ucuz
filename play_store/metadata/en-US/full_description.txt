FridgeChef is a meal planning app designed to help you run your kitchen with more structure and less guesswork.

What you can do:
- Build breakfast, lunch, and dinner menus
- Keep your pantry list up to date
- Detect missing ingredients automatically
- Create a shopping list from planned menus
- Set meal times and reminders
- Get menu suggestions based on what is already in your pantry

FridgeChef does more than show recipes. It helps you decide what to cook, organize your meals, and understand what is missing before it is time to shop.

It is a strong fit for people who want to:
- plan meals more consistently
- turn menus into shopping lists
- reduce waste by tracking what is available
- manage kitchen routines in one place

Use FridgeChef to plan smarter and cook with less friction.
